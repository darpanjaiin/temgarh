Nearby Attractions

Lavasa

22 kms away

India's first planned hill city, Lavasa is known for its vibrant architecture, waterfront activities, and pleasant weather. Perfect for a day trip with restaurants, water sports, and scenic views.

Water Sports Dining Photography

Bhode Lake

2 km

A serene lake surrounded by the Western Ghats, offering peaceful views and perfect spots for nature photography. The walking trail to the lake itself is a beautiful experience through the local landscape.

Nature Walk Photography Scenic Views

Mhasoba Temple

2.5 km away

A local temple of cultural significance, offering a glimpse into the spiritual heritage of the region. The temple provides a peaceful atmosphere and beautiful views of the surrounding area.

Spiritual Visit Valley Views

Hanuman Mandir (Watunde)

2 km

Located in Watunde village, this temple is an important religious site for locals. Visit the temple while exploring the village life and experiencing the local culture of the region.

Temple Visit Village Life Local Culture

Temgarh River

200 metres

Just a short walk from the property, the river offers a refreshing escape. Perfect for a peaceful morning walk or evening relaxation by the flowing water. Please note: Swimming/water activities at your own risk.

Riverside Walks Nature Spot Relaxation

Amenities & Activities

Nature Retreat

Discover the Western Ghats' biodiversity at our doorstep. Enjoy our extensive nature trails, including access to the Temgarh River and Bhode Lake Walk. Take advantage of direct river access. Good for birdwatching enthusiasts, with diverse species in our surroundings. Experience the perfect balance of adventure and tranquility in nature.

Sports & Recreation

Stay active with our numerous recreational facilities. Enjoy our swimming pool with a dedicated baby pool. We have a trampoline as well. Our multi-purpose grounds feature football, volleyball, basketball, and cricket facilities. Indoor entertainment includes table tennis, badminton, pool table, and various board games and carrom.

Facilities & Amenities

Experience comfort in our well-maintained rooms with and power backup. Enjoy homely meals in our gazebo dining area. Winter evenings feature a cozy bonfire. Entertainment includes a projector with screen and music system. Our conference room is perfect for business meetings and workshops. Relax in our library, and enjoy the convenience of refrigeration facilities throughout your stay. WiFi (Jio Network) works well most of the time.

Event Spaces

Perfect for various occasions from intimate gatherings to corporate events. Our conference room accommodates up to 50 people, ideal for business meetings and workshops. Celebrate special moments with our birthday party facilities and BBQ area. Versatile spaces suitable for weddings and corporate retreats in our scenic setting.
